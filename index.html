Rigged

Gear & fishing trip log for iPhone

Rigged helps anglers catalog their fishing gear, remember their spots, and keep a trip diary — all on your own device. Photograph rods, reels, flies, and lures and track which box or vest they live in. Drop GPS pins where you fish. Log every trip with auto-filled weather and a record of each fish you land.

Frequently asked questions

Where is my data stored?

Everything — gear, spots, trips, and photos — is stored on your iPhone. There are no accounts and no server. Your data is included in your normal iPhone backups if you have those enabled.

How do I back up or export my data?

Open Settings inside the app and tap Export Data. You'll get a spreadsheet file for each category plus a combined JSON backup that you can save to Files, AirDrop to a computer, or email to yourself.

Can I edit my data on a computer?

Yes. Every export includes a file called rigged-editor.html. Open it in any web browser on your computer, load the exported rigged-backup.json, make your changes, save, and import the saved file back on your iPhone with Settings > Import Edited JSON. Photos and trips are never affected by an import, and nothing is ever deleted by one.

Why isn't the weather filling in automatically?

Weather auto-fill needs three things: a GPS tag on the trip, location permission for the app, and a network connection. If any of those is missing you can always enter the conditions by hand.

Can I use metric units?

Yes. Turn on metric units in the app's Settings and fish sizes, lure weights, and temperatures will display and enter in centimeters, kilograms, grams, and Celsius.

How do I delete my data?

Delete individual items by swiping on them in any list. To remove everything, delete the app — because all data lives on your device, deleting the app removes it.
