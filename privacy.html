Privacy Policy

Rigged for iPhone · Effective July 8, 2026

Rigged is built on a simple principle: your fishing life is yours. The app has no accounts, no sign-in, no analytics, no advertising, and no server operated by us. We do not collect, see, sell, or share anything you put into the app.

What the app stores, and where

Everything you enter — gear, storage locations, fishing spots, trips, catches, notes, and photos — is stored in a database on your device. It is included in your normal device backups (iCloud Backup or computer backups) according to your own iOS settings. If a future version adds optional iCloud sync, your data would sync through your personal, private iCloud database under your Apple ID; it would remain inaccessible to us.

Location

With your permission, Rigged uses your location to tag fishing spots, trips, and catches with GPS coordinates. Those coordinates are saved on your device alongside the rest of your data. The app only accesses location while you are using it, and only when you ask it to — dropping a pin, using the My Location button, or starting a new trip log.

Weather auto-fill

When you use weather auto-fill on a trip, the app sends that trip's coordinates to a weather service — Apple Weather or Open-Meteo — solely to fetch current conditions. The coordinates are sent at reduced precision, the response is stored on your device, and we receive nothing. Those services process the request under their own policies: Apple Weather and Open-Meteo.

Camera and photos

With your permission, the app uses your camera and photo library so you can attach photos to gear, spots, trips, and catches. Attached photos are copied into the app's database on your device. Photos you export or share go only where you send them.

Export and import

Exporting your data is entirely user-initiated. Export files (spreadsheets, JSON, and shared trip images) leave the app only through the iOS share sheet, to destinations you choose. Importing an edited file changes only the data on your device.

Children

Rigged does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children. There is nothing in the app that transmits personal information to us.

Changes to this policy

If the app's data practices ever change — for example, when optional iCloud sync is added — this policy will be updated here first, and the effective date above will change.
